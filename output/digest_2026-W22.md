In a randomised, placebo-controlled, crossover trial, we here explore the safety and tolerability of, as well as any metabolic responses to, a duodenal infusion of recombinant RORDEP1 (r-RORDEP1) given to healthy men after oral intake of a liquid mixed meal. Seventeen healthy, normal-weight men between 18 and 35 years of age were randomised through block randomisation to receive either r-RORDEP1 or placebo as the initial intervention at Gentofte Hospital, Denmark. Blocks were created prior to trial initiation. Both participants and investigators were blinded to treatment.
